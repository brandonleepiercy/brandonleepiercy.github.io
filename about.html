About Me

My name is Brandon Piercy and I am a 22 year old UCSB Alumni born in San Jose, California. When I first arrived at UC Santa Barbara, I wasn't sure about what I wanted to study. Instead of going undeclared, I decided to search for a program that would be beneficial regardless of my career path, and that is how I found Communication. I realized early on that Communication plays a massive role in any type of social sitaution and being able to communicate effectively would be one the most universally important skills I could develop. During my graduation year, 2020, I lost my offer of employment from a commercial real estate firm because of the pandemic. I was initially upset, but I quickly realized that this can be an opportunity to try to enter a new field and develop an even richer skillset. I decided I would pursue software engineering, because of the overwhelming demand, my personal interest in the subject, and the challenge it presents me on a daily basis. Everything is being digitized and coding will be the language of the future, I knew I had to become fluent while it is still an option and not a requirement.
